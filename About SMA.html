SAINT MARY’S ACADEMY of Las Piñas City is a private Catholic school founded on 08 September 2005. It is inspired by faith in God, faith in the teachings and values of Jesus, and the presence of the Holy Spirit among us. It is Filipino in its pride in national identity and culture, in its continuing efforts to stimulate its students to dedicate their talents towards moral transformation of the country.

 

It aims to provide and promote Personalized Education to its students in a homey atmosphere where learning can be a great fun. It exists to make low-cost but quality education accessible to growing population.

 

We are happy to have your child enrolled at SAINT MARY’S ACADEMY of Las Piñas City! As part of SMA family, we would like to keep you always aware of what is happening to your child while at school, our programs, activities, rules and regulations. We also encourage you parents/guardians to express your concern/suggestion (if any) by dropping your note in our CONCERN BOX or you may bring this up to the attention of the Management through writing on the Diary. We appreciate comments and suggestions as these can be of great help for the improvement of your child’s “Second Home”.
