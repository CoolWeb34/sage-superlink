We, in SAINT MARY’S ACADEMY of Las Piñas City are bound by a commitment to provide a quality and affordable “Personalizes Education” to its students.

 

We, in SAINT MARY’S ACADEMY of Las Piñas City envision ourselves in spreading the “Christian Spirit, Values and Way of Life” in the different communities.

 

 

 

 

 

Specific Objectives:

 

  1. To teach its students practice Christian values in their life.
  2. To help its students develop a positive self concept.
  3. To provide its students a holistic education in a friendly environment.

 

 

PHILOSOPHY

 

A good educational foundation helps the child build a positive self concept thereby enhancing self confidence at an early age. Confidence in a child brings about high academic achievements and consequently a good future.
